Bug #3 (RESOLVED)

Due to us utilising the Pacstall development branches for Rhino Linux 2023.1-beta4 and below, there are some breakage when it comes to upgrading your pacscripts with rhino-pkg while Pacstall 4.0.0, however thankfully there is an easy fix to this. You can switch from the pacstall:develop branch to pacstall:master with pacstall -U pacstall:master, and then after that you will be able to update your system as normal with rpk update -y.
